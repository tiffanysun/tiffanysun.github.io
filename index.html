About

I find the right problem before anyone solves the wrong one.

I'm a UX & design strategist based in Oakland, CA, with a Masters in Digital Experience Design from Hyper Island. I was one of the first designers in theCamp creative residency in the south of France.

I apply design thinking to digital products and services — leading research, setting direction, and bringing stakeholders along. I care about playful interactions and frameworks that make ambiguity workable. I've worked across Apple, Capital One, BBC, and the NHS, and I speak and run workshops on design thinking and creativity.
